Simi Valley is a surburban town about 40 minutes outside of Los Angeles. It has rolling hills with fun hiking and mountain biking trails. It is a quiet family town. The average household income is $102,600 which is higher then the state average of $91,900.

One thing Simi Valley is known for is the Ronald Reagan Library. This is where Ronald Reagan was burried. This building also includes airforce 1.
