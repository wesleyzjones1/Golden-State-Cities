Sacramento is the capital of California. It is a beautiful city with water canals flowing through it. It has the 3rd largest population count in California with a population of 528,000. The average household income is $84,000 which is lower then the California average of $91,900.

You can go to the state capital building in Sacramento. This is not only an active government building but it is also a museum!
